## Equations

1. v$_f$ = v$_i$ + at
2. d = $\frac{1}{2}$ (v$_i$ + v$_f$)t
3. v = $\frac{1}{2}$ (v$_i$ + v$_f$)
4. d = v$_i$t + $\frac{1}{2}$at$^2$
5. v$_f$$^2$ = v$_i$$^2$ + 2ad OR d = $\frac{(v_f^2 - v_i^2)}{2a}$
