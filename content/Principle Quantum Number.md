## What is it

n = maximum # of sublevels that can exist for a given energy level (i.e how many types of orbitals are allowed in an energy level)

n$^2$ = maximum number of orbitals that can exist for a given energy level

2n$^2$ = maximum number of electrons that can occupy a given energy level
